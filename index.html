The Keys at Quinta

The Keys is an exclusive luxury development consisting of 48 individual villas and 24 outstanding apartments. A beautiful gated community set on the edge of the Ria Formosa Natural Park located in the ultra exclusive area of Quinta Do Lago, Portugal. The Keys development has an early 20th Century American waterway feel with fantastic contemporary properties with a 'Deco' quality to them. All of the villas and penthouse apartments have a private elevator to their own secluded sky gardens with rooftop pools. The properties range from three to six bedrooms with cinemas and multi level entertaining spaces.
AVAILABLE EARLY 2016.
