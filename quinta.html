Quinta Do Lago

Established in the early 1970's by Brazillian property developer Andre Jordan, Quinta Do Lago has grown into one of the most exclusive resorts in europe and is part of the Algarve's infamous 'Golden Triangle', consisting of Villamoura, Almancil, and Quinta Do Lago at it's points. Boasting luxury hotels, Michelin starred restaurants, bars, and cafe's in the area, not to mention the designer clothes, jewellery, and watch stores Quinta truly has it all for the wealthy shopper. The Portugese open has been held at Quinta several times on the world class golf courses of which there are many. The area has a vast amount of multi million euro properties spread throuout various golf courses and gated communities and is a favourite destination of the rich and famous who can regularly be seen frequenting the local restaurants and bars. British household names including Chris Evans, Bradley Walsh, Philip Schofield, Holly Willoughby and Ant & Dec are a few of the regulars.
